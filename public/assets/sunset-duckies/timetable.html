Heat format Heats of 4 · 10 min

  • 4 surfers per heat · colour rashies (red · yellow · blue · green)
  • 10 minutes, unlimited waves, best 2 count
  • Each wave: 5 tick boxes — Paddle & commit · Pop-up · Stance · Ride · Stoke
  • Max 10 ticks per surfer
  • Tiebreaker 1: total waves attempted
  • Tiebreaker 2: judges' "wave of the heat" shaka 🤙
  • Bias toward yes — when in doubt, tick the box
