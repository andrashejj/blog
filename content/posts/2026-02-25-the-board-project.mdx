After the first post about craft and how this whole thing started, I sat down and tried to map out what we are actually getting into.

The board is ordered. The foam blank takes about 8 weeks to arrive. That gives us time to prepare before we touch anything, and a few weeks after to actually shape, paint, and finish it. Twelve weeks total.

Noah is 8. So this needs to be fun, not a curriculum. But I also want us to take it seriously enough that we actually learn something real. Not just show up and watch.

The first thing we did was call the guys from [Santosha](https://santoshasurf.com/). We talked about Noah first, then about the project. We discussed the rough shape of it and agreed that we could probably be much more involved than we initially thought.
