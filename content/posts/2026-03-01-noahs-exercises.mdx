I used to draw these worksheets by hand every single day for Noah. It was a fun morning ritual, but some days it was tough to come up with new math problems and creative writing prompts before the coffee kicked in.

Now, I've digitized the whole process, making it significantly easier for everyone. This comprehensive template incorporates physical, mathematical, creative writing, and drawing exercises into a single interactive flow. Best of all, the math problems can be regenerated infinitely with a click, and the daily writing hooks are powered by an AI on the server so they are always fresh.

Simply pull up this page on an iPad or print it out, and the daily workout is ready to go.
