One thing I've learned coaching kids at Tamarin Bay: what you do on the sand matters just as much as what happens in the water. The best surf sessions start with structured warm-ups that build the exact movement patterns kids need — pop-ups, balance, hip rotation, paddle endurance.

This is our growing collection of land-based exercises. Each one is tagged by type, format, and body focus so you can quickly build a session plan. Filter by what you need: a group warm-up for mobility, a pairs drill for balance, or an individual strength exercise. Print it out and bring it to the beach.
